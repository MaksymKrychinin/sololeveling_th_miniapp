## 🎯 In 60 Seconds

### What is it?
Gamified habit tracking Telegram Mini App where users complete daily quests, level up, and unlock achievements - inspired by Solo Leveling anime.

### What works NOW?
✅ Login via Telegram  
✅ View daily quests  
✅ Complete quests  
✅ Gain XP & level up  
✅ Build streaks  
✅ Unlock achievements
